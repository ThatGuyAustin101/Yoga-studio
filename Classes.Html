Although designed for intermediate to advvanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-sychronized movement-you will inhale and exhale as you flow energentically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minhute class features very slow menement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, rstorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.

Relax Anytime with Savasana

When you are ready roll on your side and use your arms yourself to a sitting position with crossed legs. Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life. Namaste."
